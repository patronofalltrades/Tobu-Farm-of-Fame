## Environment notes

- The preview tab runs hidden, so rAF-based fps sampling only captures frames during forced render bursts; the median (16.8 ms) is representative, tail percentiles are not.
- 3D tap targets were driven by synthetic PointerEvents with `offsetX/offsetY` defined canvas-relative (R3F raycasts from offset coords; synthetic events default these to 0).
